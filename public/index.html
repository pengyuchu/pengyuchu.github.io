Hello there!

I am a Ph.D. candidate advised by Dr. Zhaojian Li in the RIVAL group at Michigan State University. My primary focus is to develop adaptive perception algorithms for robotics. Based on this goal, my research resolves around image segmentation and vision-based localization in orchard-grown fruit harvesting, visual reasoning as well as reinforcement learning. I am being expected to graduate in Dec, 2023. Previously, I obtained my M.S. in Computer Science at Michigan State University and B.S. in Computer Science at Sichuan University.
